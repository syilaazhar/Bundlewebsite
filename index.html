ABOUT US


Welcome To Skool Bundle !!!

Hello and welcome to our website this is the part where we will inform you about ourselves. The store was founded by 4 entrepreneur which go back to as early as 2019. The store was originally based on a store in Klang. We specialized in selling used outfits that can be found at a very reasonable price. We got the inspiration of opening this store is because we want to avoid textile waste which was around 13 million tons during 2017. We decide to make sure that this clothing can still be use and to avoid people not wanting it we decided to put a very reasonable price on the clothing to make sure it would not go to waste.


The name Skool Bundle came from an inspiration from the word school so that the kind of outfit are mostly used by teenagers and making sure that our target audience are satisfied with the price and the quality that they will receive.


All our materials are guaranteed to be original because before we decide to put up on our store we will go through each material before putting a price tag on it and by doing so it will make lots of people be more confident in buying our stuff.
